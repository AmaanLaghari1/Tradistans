What is Tradistans?

This is a product given by Trading Consultancy that offers an interest rate premium in exchange for a specified quantity of funds placed by a customer for a set of length of time. Every withdrawal has traditional terms, tenure, rates, and applicable penalties.

  • Tradistans is Secured, Convenient and Easy to use.
  • You can earn by reffering your Friends and Family.

How does Tradistans Works?

Tradistans are made up of the following tenures:

  • Interest Rate: Fixed rates that provide clear and predictable return on your deposit over a set length of time
  • Term/Tenure: The amount of time you agree to leave your funds deposited in order to avoid penalties
  • Principal: The money you come up depositing when you sign up for the Tradistans
